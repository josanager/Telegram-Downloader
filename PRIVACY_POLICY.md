# Privacy Policy - Telegram Video Downloader Pro

**Effective Date: February 6, 2024**

Telegram Video Downloader Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy describes our practices regarding the collection and use of information.

### 1. Information Collection
**We do not collect any personal information.**
The extension operates entirely on your local machine. We do not have access to your Telegram account, your phone number, your contacts, or the content of your messages.

### 2. User Data Handling
Telegram Video Downloader Pro reads the URLs of video and image files within the Telegram Web interface for the sole purpose of allowing you to download them to your computer.
- **No Remote Storage**: No data is uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.
- **No Tracking**: We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies to monitor your activity.

### 3. Data Processing (Byte-Relay)
The extension uses a "Byte-Relay" mechanism to facilitate downloads. This process creates a temporary local buffer of video data in your browser's memory. This data is discarded immediately after the file is saved to your 'Downloads' folder.

### 4. Third-Party Access
There is no third-party access to any part of the extension's logic or the data it processes.

### 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the support link provided in the Chrome Web Store.
